RECIPE FOR PASTA

INGREDIENTS:

  • For Pasta
    • Pasta -> 200g
    • Salt -> for boiling
    • Water -> as needed
  • For Sauce
    • Cream -> 1 cup
    • Garlic -> 2 cloves
    • Olive oil -> 1 tbsp
    • Pepper -> to taste
    • Cheese -> optional

STEPS:

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Add pasta and cook until al dente.
  3. Drain the pasta and keep it aside.
  4. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic.
  5. Add vegetables or meat as desired and cook for 3-5 minutes.
  6. Add cream or tomato sauce and mix well.
  7. Toss the pasta in the sauce and serve hot with cheese.

SOME TIPS

  1. Use freshly grated cheese for better taste and creaminess.

  2. Add vegetables like bell peppers, mushrooms, or spinach to make it healthier.

  3. Sprinkle fresh herbs like parsley or basil on top before serving.
