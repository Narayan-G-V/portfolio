RECIPE FOR PIZZA

INGREDIENTS:

  • Dough
    • All-purpose flour -> 2 cups
    • Yeast -> 1 tsp
    • Sugar -> 1 tsp
    • Salt -> 1 tsp
    • Water -> 3/4 cup
  • Toppings
    • Tomato sauce -> as needed
    • Cheese -> as desired
    • Vegetables (bell peppers, onions, tomatoes) -> as desired
    • Meats (optional) -> as desired

STEPS:

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circular shape.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add toppings like vegetables, cheese, and meats.
  5. Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden.
  6. Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot.

SOME TIPS

  1. Preheat the oven properly to get a crispy crust.

  2. Use fresh mozzarella or a mix of cheeses for authentic taste.

  3. Dont overload with toppings; it may make the pizza soggy.
