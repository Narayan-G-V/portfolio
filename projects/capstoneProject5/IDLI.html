RECIPE FOR IDLI

INGREDIENTS:

  • Batter
    • Rice -> 2 cups
    • Urad dal -> 1 cup
    • Water -> as needed
    • Salt -> to taste

STEPS:

  1. Take some rice and urad dal in a bowl.
  2. Soak them in water for 6-8 hours.
  3. Grind them to a smooth batter.
  4. Mix the batter and let it ferment overnight.
  5. Grease the idli molds and pour the batter.
  6. Steam the idlis for 10-15 minutes until cooked.
  7. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.

SOME TIPS

  1. Add a little fenugreek seeds while grinding the batter for extra flavor and softer idlis.

  2. Ensure the batter is fermented properly overnight for fluffy idlis.

  3. Grease the idli molds lightly to prevent sticking.
